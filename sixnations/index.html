FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Six Nations Championship?

The Six Nations Championship is the annual international rugby union competition between England, France, Ireland, Italy, Scotland, and Wales. Founded in 1883 as the Home Nations Championship, it is the oldest rugby tournament in the world and one of the most-watched sporting events in Europe.

How can I follow Six Nations matches live?

The 6 Nations app delivers real-time score updates, push notifications for tries and conversions, and iOS Live Activities so you can follow every match directly from your lock screen or Dynamic Island. You can also check fixtures, kickoff times, and TV broadcast information.

Which devices support the 6 Nations app?

The app is available on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Apple Vision Pro. It includes a home screen widget for quick match countdowns and Live Activities for real-time match tracking on iOS 16 and later.

Is the 6 Nations app free?

Yes, the 6 Nations app is completely free to download and use. All features including live scores, fixtures, standings, push notifications, and the match countdown widget are included at no cost.
