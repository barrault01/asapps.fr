Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026

RewriteMe is provided by Antoine Barrault. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information You Provide

RewriteMe processes the text you type, paste, or dictate so it can rewrite, adjust tone, or translate the result. The app does not operate an account system and does not require you to provide your name, email address, or profile information.

On-Device Processing

RewriteMe is designed to use Apple platform capabilities and on-device app features where available. Drafts and rewritten text are not sent to servers operated by Antoine Barrault.

Dictation and Speech Recognition

If you use dictation, audio is processed through Apple speech recognition features available on your device. Your use of Apple speech recognition is subject to Apple privacy settings and policies.

Third-Party Services

RewriteMe does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

Data Storage

Text you enter and generated results are handled inside the app session. The app does not create a cloud account or upload your writing to a developer-operated backend.

Diagnostics

If the app crashes, Apple may collect crash diagnostics if you have enabled sharing diagnostics with app developers in iOS settings. These diagnostics are provided by Apple and do not include developer-operated tracking.

Children's Privacy

RewriteMe is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated as RewriteMe changes. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact asapp@me.com.
