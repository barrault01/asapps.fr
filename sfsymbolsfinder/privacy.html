Privacy Policy

Your symbol searches stay private.

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Ultimate SF Symbols finder is developed by Antoine Barrault. This policy explains how the app handles information. The short version: the app does not collect personal data, does not use advertising, and does not send analytics to us.

No account is required. You can browse, search, save favorites, keep recent symbols, and copy symbol names without signing in.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not operate a backend service for this app and we do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. The app does not include:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Third-party analytics SDKs
  • User accounts or registration
  • Server-side tracking controlled by us

Local Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores small pieces of information locally using Apple system storage, such as:

  • Your favorite symbols
  • Your recent symbols
  • Your preferred search language
  • Your display and grid preferences

This information is used only to make the app work better for you. You can remove it by deleting the app from your device.

Translation

The app can use Apple's system Translation features to help search in multiple languages. Translation requests are handled by Apple system APIs when available. We do not receive your search queries, translated text, or language preferences.

Clipboard

When you tap a symbol, the app copies the symbol name to your clipboard. Clipboard access is only used for the copy action you request.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@asapps.fr.
