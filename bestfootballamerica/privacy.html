Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to BestFootball - America 2021, an iOS app published by Antoine Barrault.

I use the information described below to provide the app, maintain app quality, and respond to support requests. I do not sell personal information.

Information Processed by the App

  • App preferences, favorites, notification choices, and similar settings may be stored on your device.
  • If a version of the app includes predictions, leagues, or saved tournament activity, that activity may be processed to provide those features.
  • If you contact support, I receive the contact details and message content you choose to send.

Diagnostics, Ads, and Store Services

The app may use Apple services for App Store distribution, purchases, crash reports, and diagnostics. Some versions may use AdMob or similar advertising services, which can process device and usage information under their own policies.

AdMob information: Google partner sites policy.

How Information Is Used

  • To deliver scores, fixtures, standings, alerts, and app settings.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.
  • To answer support requests and comply with App Store requirements.

Third-Party Data

Sports scores, fixtures, standings, and related content may come from third-party providers. Their services can change independently of the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through support contact, please contact me so I can delete it.

Changes

I may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.
